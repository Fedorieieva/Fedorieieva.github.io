About Us

Our mission is to find loving homes for all of the animals in our care. We believe that every pet deserves a second chance, and we are committed to providing them with the care and attention they need to thrive.

We are a non-profit organization that relies on donations and volunteers to keep our doors open. We are always looking for new volunteers to help us with everything from cleaning and feeding the animals to socializing them and finding them homes.

Our History

Our shelter was founded in 1990 by a group of animal lovers who were concerned about the number of homeless pets in our community. We started out as a small operation, but we have grown over the years to become one of the largest pet shelters in the area.

Our Goals

Our goal is to find loving homes for all of the animals in our care. We also want to educate the public about the importance of spaying and neutering their pets and about the benefits of adopting a pet from a shelter.
